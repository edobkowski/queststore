Hello, Codecooler!

Welcome to the QuestStore© platform

QuestStore is a system designed for Codecoolers, who are the students of the Codecool© programming school. It allows them to spend their hard earned CoolCoins©, which is the name of the Coodecool virtual currency. Codecoolers earn CoolCoins for different achievements during their education and can buy various privileges or perks by paying with them.

The store is the platform where you can use your CoolCoins to buy certain things. The store items do not have any material value they represent activities or events that enhance the Codecool experience. The store offers two type of items: basic and extra (magic). Assortment and prices can be changed at the beggining of the week, depending on demand and supply.

If you are Codecooler log in to the platform and try it out by yourself. Be careful, do not waste your Coolcoins hastily. If you don't have active account, write to your Mentor in order to get the access to the platform.
